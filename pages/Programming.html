I am a beginner in the field of programming. I have seen many resources for starting to learn to code. However, the problem lies after this step. I can't seem to generate ideas for apps, websites, games and so on. If I do get an idea, I am not able to implement it properly. This section is a way, I believe, to address this problem.

This won't be a complete curriculum on programming because I feel there are already many courses available online which are made by people way better than me. This will be a place, where I share my creation process, starting from the idea generation, design to publishing my product.I will decide the theme, the kind of product (mobile app or game or desktop software),the framework and the programming language I am going to use. I will post them by Monday. Every following night, I will post my progress. Hopefully by the end of the week, I believe I will complete the project.

You can find all my project files in my github page. If required, I will link to external resources.If you are new to programming, please start by reading the post "How to Start". You are always welcomed to post your suggestions. I am also excited to see your projects, so if you do try them, post a link to your github repo in the comments.
